About Me

Damien is a software engineer and web developer with a passion for UX/UI design, site accessibility, and performance optimization. He graduated from the University of California - Santa Barbara with bachelors degrees in both Physics and Philosophy. Damien is also an entrepreneur, teacher, documentary filmmaker, amateur photographer, and a social justice & climate change activist. In his free time, he spends time with his two cats and two dogs and enjoys gardening, weight lifting, learning, watching/playing football and soccer, coding, and making the world a better place.
